Glass Days

A yearly meeting where glass science, education, craftsmanship and innovation come together.

The Glass Days Story

The Glass Days are organized annually by the Union for Glass Science and Technology (USTV) and the Lycée Jean Monnet – École Nationale du Verre. They provide a unique opportunity for researchers, teachers, students and glass professionals to exchange ideas.
