ANR 3VER

Towards Greener Glass

Developing low-carbon glass compositions using recycled materials and volcanic resources.

Project Overview

The ANR 3VER project aims to develop innovative low-carbon glasses by combining recycled glass, alternative mineral resources and a better understanding of glass structure and processing.

Scientific Objectives

Recycled Glass

Increase the use of secondary raw materials in glass production.

Volcanic Resources

Explore volcanic materials as alternatives to conventional carbonate sources.

Glass Properties

Understand viscosity, structure and processing conditions.
